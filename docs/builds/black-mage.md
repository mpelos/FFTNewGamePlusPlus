# Black Mage Build Notes

Source basis: internet research only.

## Best Reference Build

Game8 recommends Black Mage with Arithmeticks, Mana Shield, Magick Boost, and Manafont. Their job-combo
guide specifically highlights Black Mage + Arithmeticks because Black Mage has top generic MA for
map-wide spell damage.

## Ability Package

```text
Primary: Black Magick
Secondary: Arithmeticks or Iaido
Reaction: Mana Shield or Shirahadori
Support: Magick Boost
Movement: Manafont or Teleport
```

## NG++ Enemy Adaptation

Black Mage should usually be a clear priority target. Swiftness/Swiftspell-style support, Mana Shield,
and Manafont make it more urgent, but keep charge-time and line-of-approach counterplay readable. Iaido
secondary is a strong alternative when you want instant MA-scaling area pressure without full
Arithmeticks.
