# Chemist Build Notes

Source basis: internet research only.

## Best Reference Build

Game8 recommends Chemist as an elemental gun or Stoneshooter unit with Items, Arts of War secondary,
Shirahadori, and high mobility. The key combo is ranged item utility plus ranged equipment/stat breaks
from gun range.

## Ability Package

```text
Primary: Items
Secondary: Arts of War
Reaction: Shirahadori
Support: Magick Boost for elemental guns, or Reequip for Stoneshooter
Movement: Movement +3
```

## NG++ Enemy Adaptation

Chemist is strongest as a backline support/ranged breaker. Use full Item access when the encounter can
handle revive/heal pressure; otherwise restrict Items to Potion/Hi-Potion/Remedy to prevent long
cleanup. Arts of War pairs well with guns when a battle wants ranged debuff pressure.
