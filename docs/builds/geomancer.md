# Geomancer Build Notes

Source basis: internet research only.

## Best Reference Build

Game8 recommends a mixed offensive Geomancer with Geomancy, Items secondary, Nature's Wrath, Magick
Boost, and Teleport. A physical variant uses Attack Boost and Bracers to push weapon damage.

## Ability Package

```text
Primary: Geomancy
Secondary: Items
Reaction: Nature's Wrath
Support: Magick Boost or Attack Boost
Movement: Teleport or Movement +3
```

## NG++ Enemy Adaptation

Geomancer is ideal as a hybrid terrain bruiser. Use Magick Boost for terrain/status pressure, Attack
Boost for weapon threat, and Nature's Wrath when you want melee attackers to risk counter-chip. It is
strong but readable because terrain and positioning explain its output.
