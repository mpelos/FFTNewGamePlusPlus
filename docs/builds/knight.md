# Knight Build Notes

Source basis: internet research only.

## Best Reference Build

Game8 recommends a Dual Wield-focused Knight using Arts of War, Fundaments, Mana Shield, Dual Wield,
and Manafont. The build doubles attacks and can apply Arts of War twice, with Fundaments chosen mainly
for Focus and PA scaling.

## Ability Package

```text
Primary: Arts of War
Secondary: Fundaments
Reaction: Mana Shield
Support: Dual Wield
Movement: Manafont
```

## NG++ Enemy Adaptation

Knight is a strong front-line anchor. For fair enemy design, cap Rend sources per fight and decide
whether the Knight is a breaker, tank, or bodyguard. Defense Boost/Parry-style setups are better when
the battle should tax positioning rather than punish gear.
