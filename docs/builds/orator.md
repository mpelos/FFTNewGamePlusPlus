# Orator Build Notes

Source basis: internet research only.

## Best Reference Build

Game8 recommends Orator as an elemental gun or Stoneshooter unit with Speechcraft, Arts of War
secondary, Shirahadori, and high movement. The build mirrors Chemist's ranged break logic while keeping
Speechcraft utility.

## Ability Package

```text
Primary: Speechcraft
Secondary: Arts of War
Reaction: Shirahadori
Support: Magick Boost or Reequip
Movement: Movement +3
```

## NG++ Enemy Adaptation

Orator is best as ranged soft-control and Brave/Faith manipulation. Use it to amplify a main threat,
not as a hard-lock caster. Gun + Arts of War is a strong variant when the battle supports ranged break
pressure.
