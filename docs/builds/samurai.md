# Samurai Build Notes

Source basis: internet research only.

## Best Reference Build

Game8 recommends Samurai with Iaido, Arts of War secondary, Shirahadori, Dual Wield, and Movement +3.
It also presents an Iaido-focused variant with Magick Boost and Teleport. Dual Wield is preferred over
Doublehand for spreading damage across two attempts.

## Ability Package

```text
Primary: Iaido
Secondary: Arts of War or Items
Reaction: Shirahadori
Support: Dual Wield or Magick Boost
Movement: Movement +3 or Teleport
```

## NG++ Enemy Adaptation

Samurai can be either a parry screen or an MA-scaling area threat. Shirahadori makes physical rushes
bad, while Iaido gives instant area pressure. Magick Boost pushes Iaido; Dual Wield pushes weapon
damage.
