# Squire Build Notes

Source basis: internet research only.

## Best Reference Build

Game8 recommends an offensive Squire built around dual Icebrands, Arts of War secondary, Mana Shield,
Dual Wield, and Manafont. The idea is to turn a normally basic job into a fast physical attacker with
utility break access and MP-buffer survival.

## Ability Package

```text
Primary: Fundaments
Secondary: Arts of War
Reaction: Mana Shield
Support: Dual Wield
Movement: Manafont
```

## NG++ Enemy Adaptation

Use Squire as a flexible physical utility unit rather than as a pure stat stick. For enemies, the
strongest fair version is a PA-focused unit with Fundaments plus either Arts of War or Items. Avoid
stacking too many Squires with Rend-style pressure unless the battle is explicitly about break control.
