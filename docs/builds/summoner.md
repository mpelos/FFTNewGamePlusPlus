# Summoner Build Notes

Source basis: internet research only.

## Best Reference Build

Game8 recommends Summoner with Summon, Arithmeticks secondary, Shirahadori, Magick Boost, and Teleport.
Their combo guide notes Summoner + Arithmeticks is less raw MA than Black Mage but more flexible
because Summon remains useful outside Arithmeticks.

## Ability Package

```text
Primary: Summon
Secondary: Arithmeticks or Time Magick
Reaction: Shirahadori
Support: Magick Boost or Swiftspell
Movement: Teleport
```

## NG++ Enemy Adaptation

Summoner should be an area threat that rewards movement and target priority. Swiftspell/Swiftness
shortens the clock; Time Magick or White Magick secondary adds support. Avoid instant unavoidable
summon walls unless the encounter is explicitly a caster spike.
