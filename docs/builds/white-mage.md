# White Mage Build Notes

Source basis: internet research only.

## Best Reference Build

Game8 recommends an Arithmeticks-focused White Mage with White Magick, Arithmeticks secondary, Mana
Shield, Magick Boost, and Manafont. The build uses MA gear for map-wide spell output while retaining
healing and Holy access.

## Ability Package

```text
Primary: White Magick
Secondary: Arithmeticks
Reaction: Mana Shield
Support: Magick Boost
Movement: Manafont
```

## NG++ Enemy Adaptation

White Mage is most dangerous when it turns sustain into a second objective. For enemy design, decide
whether Raise/Arise is allowed; if not, keep it to Cure, Protect, Shell, Esuna, and Holy. Mana Shield
plus Manafont is durable but fair if the player can pressure MP or Silence.
